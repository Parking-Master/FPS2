Room Rules

Welcome to the world chat! This chat room will allow you to send, receive, and view messages/invites from other players!

Here's the rules for what you're NOT allowed to do in this room:
  • Don't be dirty. Messages that include profanity or offend other users will be blocked by an AI, or your account will possibly be restricted.

  • Don't be mean. You should treat others how you like to be treated. Try welcoming other players and share some game invites!

  • Use good grammar. Your messages syntax should be nice and clean for others to read. Messages that are not worthy of posting will be possibly hidden.
Here's the rules for what you ARE allowed to do in this room:
  • Invite some players for a 1v1 match!

  • Be funny! Post some memes and upload some funny pictures.

  • Follow the room rules (obviously)
